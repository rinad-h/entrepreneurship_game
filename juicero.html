Autopsy Report

Juicero

Time of Death: September 2017

Background Check

Juicero was founded in 2013 and launched its high-tech juicer in 2016. The company aimed to revolutionize the juice industry with a premium, connected appliance that used proprietary juice packs. Marketing focused on convenience and quality, targeting health-conscious consumers willing to pay for fresh, made-to-order juice.

Cause of Death

Juicero faced backlash for its high price and the revelation that the juice packs could be squeezed by hand, making the expensive machine largely unnecessary. Consumer interest never met expectations, and the company shut down in September 2017.

Underlying Conditions

  • Overpriced product with limited perceived value.
  • Core technology (juice packs) could be used without the machine.
  • Target market was too niche to sustain growth.
  • Heavy reliance on hype rather than clear consumer benefit.

Autopsy Findings

  • Premium pricing must be justified by clear, unique value.
  • Products should solve real consumer problems, not just be flashy or tech-driven.
  • Niche markets need careful validation before scaling.

Preventative Measures

  • Conduct thorough market research to confirm demand and willingness to pay.
  • Test prototypes to ensure core features deliver real benefits.
  • Focus on differentiating features that cannot be easily bypassed or replicated.
